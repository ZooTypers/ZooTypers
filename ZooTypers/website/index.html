Our product is an animal themed typing game. Using the android keyboard, users will type words as they appear on the screen in order to attain points. Our product is interesting because we make this educational, typing application into an enjoyable and addicting game, allowing users to choose their own animal avatars and backgrounds. Therefore, our target audience is large, including both adults and children alike. Furthermore, we will make revenue for our product by adding an advertisement banner to the main screen and creating unique animal avatars & backgrounds that require minor payments. Ultimately, the game will attract anyone who wants to improve their spelling skills and typing speed on mobile devices in an entertaining way.
